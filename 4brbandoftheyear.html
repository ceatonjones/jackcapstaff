Derwent Brass - Nominated 4Barsrest 'Band of the Year' 2022

Jack and Derwent Brass reflect on the nomiation to be crowned the 4barsrest 'Band of the Year', in exceptionally good company with some of the world's very best brass bands.

Speaking on the Success, Jack commented:


I'm absolutely delighted to see Derwent Brass nominated for the 4barsrest 'Band of the Year' 2022 award, among some extremely fine company.

We have a band who's ambition and fervour in pushing the boundaries of the brass band medium truly does deserve recognition; and nomination, regardless of result, is a real testament to the group's continued musical growth and success.

What an organisation to be a part of.


Band spokesperson, Chris Leonard, says:

We are absolutely thrilled to have been nominated for 4barsrest 'Band of the Year' 2022, sandwiched between some of the most incredible bands in the movement! Wow; it really is an honour.

The band's reputation to stretch, push and succeed artistically has really found some traction this year and we thank all those followers and people who have supported our journey and helped make it resonate.

Of course none of this would be possible without the sheer committment and determination of our Music Director Jack Capstaff and everyone that is Derwent Brass. #TeamDB
