Tuition

Jack has the firm belief that music should be available to all who wish to experience it. Anyone who wishes to learn the craft of music should be afforded the opportunity to, and it is with this thought in mind that Jack lends himself tuition of music in all manners and styles.

Be it music theory, conducting, composition, the art of performance, collaboration or the production of music; Jack believes in the transference of skills from one genre of music to another. Whilst the variety of music has never been greater than today, there lies fundamental techniques which can be create a basis for any musician to find their style.

Jack has helped to enable musicians of all abilities, ages and styles from young learners of brass instruments and dance music production to people learning music theory in later life. There is no right or wrong time to start learning music and no point at which learning stops, so should you wish to talk with Jack, please contact him through the contact page.
