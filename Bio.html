Biography

Jack Capstaff is an up and coming conductor and composer originally from York.

He studied at Huntington School under the keen eye of Elizabeth Dunbar before attending the Royal Welsh College of Music and Drama with support from tutors in composition including Dr Robert Spearing and tuition in conducting.  

Jack currently lives in York but travels across the country for engagements.
