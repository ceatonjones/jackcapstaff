Music De Los Muertos Hits the Right Note

Having presented Musica De Los Muertos at both Bolsover Entertainments Contest, and Wychavon Festival of Brass Entertainments Contest; Derwent secured the title of "most entertaining band" at both competitions with the brand new set of Big Band inspired editions of western classical music.
